About Me

Technology leader with a passion for innovation and entrepreneurship

Mr. Kalyanjit Hatibaruah is an accomplished technology management professional with diverse experience in technology and consulting across multidisciplinary domains. He is a well-known public speaker on Web3, Metaverse, Artificial Intelligence, Entrepreneurship, and the Startup Ecosystem.

He holds a BTech degree in Electronics & Communication from NIT Kurukshetra, Haryana, and an MBA from Pune University. With extensive experience in different technology stacks, he is recognized as a pioneer in Blockchain Applications and is involved as an Advisor or Lead Tech teams in various Financial Projects related to Blockchain and Tokenization.
