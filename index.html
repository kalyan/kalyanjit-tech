About Me

Bridging the gap between technical engineering and strategic management

Mr. Kalyanjit Hatibaruah is a distinguished technology management professional, industry-academia enabler, and Chairman of Flugelsoft Group. With over 25 years of experience at the intersection of technology and business strategy, he is recognized as a pioneer in driving the adoption of emerging technologies, including Artificial Intelligence (AI), Web3, and Blockchain.

Kalyanjit holds a B.Tech in Electronics & Communication from the National Institute of Technology (NIT) Kurukshetra, and an MBA from Pune University. This dual background allows him to translate complex AI frameworks into actionable business value for organizations and students alike.

He serves on the Advisory Board for Gujarat National Law University’s (GNLU) research project on “A Critical Analysis of the Regulatory Framework for the Crypto Assets in India” alongside eminent justices, bureaucrats, economists, lawyers, and academicians. He is also a regular contributor on Technology in the Maritime industry for The Week Online Portal.
