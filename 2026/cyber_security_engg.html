Defense in the Digital Age

Cyber Security is the practice of protecting systems, networks, and programs from digital attacks. It is the convergence of technology, people, and processes.

The CIA Triad

At the heart of every security framework lies the CIA Triad. For engineering students designing systems, balancing these three pillars is the fundamental challenge. Research often focuses on maintaining one without compromising the others.

  • C

    Confidentiality

    Preventing unauthorized access to sensitive information.

  • I

    Integrity

    Ensuring data is accurate and trustworthy; preventing tampering.

  • A

    Availability

    Ensuring authorized users have reliable access to resources.
