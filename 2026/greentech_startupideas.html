Why Build Green?

The global shift towards sustainability is not just an ethical imperative; it is the largest economic opportunity of the 21st century. For engineering students at NIT Kurukshetra, this represents a unique chance to solve hard technical problems—from energy storage to waste valorization—while building scalable businesses.

From Idea to Impact

A simplified roadmap for student founders.

01. Validation

Don't build yet. Talk to 50 potential customers. Is the pain point real?

02. MVP Prototype

Build the "Minimum Viable Product". Use low-code tools or basic prototypes to solve the core problem.

03. Pilot & Scale

Deploy in a small controlled environment (e.g., inside NIT campus). Gather data, iterate, then seek funding.
