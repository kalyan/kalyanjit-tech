🇮🇳 Support Hubs in India

Social Alpha (Clean Energy Lab)

Bengaluru, India

Focused on lab-to-market journey for deep-tech green energy and circular economy startups.

OFFERS: Labs, Seed Capital, Mentorship

Villgro (Climate Action Track)

Multi-city, India

India’s oldest social incubator. Provides up to ₹50L funding for Agri-tech and renewable startups.

OFFERS: GTM Support, Pilot Funding

Sangam AIC (Atal Innovation Centre)

Gurgaon, India

Exclusive focus on clean-tech and sustainability hardware. Proximity to NIT KKR makes it accessible.

OFFERS: Prototyping Lab, Hardware Grants

🌍 Global Accelerators

Greentown Labs

Boston / Houston, USA

The largest ClimateTech incubator in North America. Massive prototyping space for carbon tech.

OFFERS: Multi-million $ Ecosystem, Corporate Demo Days

Climate-KIC

Amsterdam / London, Europe

EU’s main climate innovation initiative. Great for circular economy and urban tech startups.

OFFERS: EU Policy Access, Cross-border Scaling

Carbon13

Cambridge, UK

The venture builder for the climate emergency. Focused on ventures reducing CO2 by 10M tonnes/yr.

OFFERS: Co-founder Matching, Pre-seed Investment
