05. Future Trajectory

We are moving from "Component Twins" (a single motor) to "Process Twins" (a manufacturing line) and finally to "System Twins" (entire cities). The integration of Generative AI is the next frontier.

Research Hotspot

"Cognitive Digital Twins"

Twins that don't just report data, but autonomously negotiate and solve problems using LLMs and Reinforcement Learning.
