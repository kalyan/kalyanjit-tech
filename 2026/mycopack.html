Mycelium spores are introduced. They act as a natural binding agent, eliminating the need for toxic resins.

Solidification

Over 5 days, the mycelium grows into the exact shape of the mold, creating a shock-absorbent matrix.

GROWTH TIME: 120 HOURS

Heat Deactivation

A brief heat cycle stops the growth and sterilizes the material, leaving a lightweight, durable packaging part.
